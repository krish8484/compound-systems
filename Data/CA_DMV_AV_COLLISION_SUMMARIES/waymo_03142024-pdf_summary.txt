On March 14, 2024 at 2:42 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in Los Angeles, California was in a collision involving a passenger car on S Figueroa Street at 7th Street. The Waymo AV was traveling northeast on S Figueroa Street in the lane adjacent to the rightmost, bus-only lane. As the Waymo AV approached the intersection with 7th Street, it came to a stop behind another vehicle stopped in traffic. As a passenger car was passing the Waymo AV in the bus lane to the right of the Waymo AV, the passenger car made contact with the passenger side of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage and the passenger car left the scene.
