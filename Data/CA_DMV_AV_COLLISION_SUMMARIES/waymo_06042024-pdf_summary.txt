On June 4, 2024 at 6:26 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a collision involving an SUV on Lombard Street at Fillmore Street. The Waymo AV was stopped in traffic for a red light on eastbound Lombard Street when an SUV traveling at a high rate of speed changed lanes and approached the Waymo AV from behind. The front of the SUV made contact with the rear of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode. Both vehicles sustained damage.
