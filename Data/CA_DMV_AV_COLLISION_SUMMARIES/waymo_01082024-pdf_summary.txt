On January 8, 2023 at 12:05 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in Los Angeles, California was in a collision involving an SUV on Manhattan Place at West Adams Boulevard. The Waymo AV was traveling northbound on Manhattan Place when it came to a stop to yield to an oncoming vehicle in a narrow roadway with parked vehicles on both sides of the street. As the oncoming SUV passed the Waymo AV, the driver’s side rear of the SUV made contact with the driver’s side rear of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage and the SUV left the scene. Print Clear Form OL 316 (REV. 7/2020) WWW
