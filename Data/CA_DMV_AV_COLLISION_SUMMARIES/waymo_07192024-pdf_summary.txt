On July 19, 2024 at 1:26 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in Mountain View, California was in a collision involving a curb in a parking lot near 65 Alta Avenue. The Waymo AV was traveling through a parking lot when it drove over a curb and the bottom of the Waymo AV made contact with the curb. At the time of the contact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was not engaged and a test driver was operating the Waymo AV in manual mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage.
