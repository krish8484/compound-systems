On June 5, 2024 at 1:12 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a collision involving a passenger car on 29th Avenue near Lawton Street. The Waymo AV was parked at the curb on the right side of 29th Avenue when a passenger car in front of the Waymo AV began to reverse to parallel park. The passenger car’s rear driver side corner made contact with the front driver side corner of the parked Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage. Print Clear Form OL 316 (REV. 7/2020) WWW
