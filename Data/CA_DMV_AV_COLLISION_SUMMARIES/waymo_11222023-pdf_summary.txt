On November 22, 2023 at 7:59 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in Los Angeles, California was in a collision involving a passenger vehicle on West 7th Street at South Park View Street. The Waymo AV was stopped for a red light facing southeast on West 7th Street at South Park View Street when a passenger vehicle approached from behind and made contact with the rear of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode, and a test driver was present (in the driver’s seating position). The Waymo AV sustained damage.
