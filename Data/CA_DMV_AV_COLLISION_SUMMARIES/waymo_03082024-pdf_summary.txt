On March 8, 2024 at 12:06 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle ("Waymo AV") operating in Los Angeles, California was in a collision involving a passenger car near the 1600 block of S. Figueroa Street. The Waymo AV made a left turn from W. 17th Street onto S. Figueroa Street and came to a stop in a queue of stopped traffic. A passenger car traveling straight on S. Figueroa Street behind the Waymo AV made contact with the rear of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV's Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage and the passenger car left the scene. Print Clear Form OL 316 (REV. 7/2020) WWW
