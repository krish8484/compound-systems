On June 11, 2024 at 12:07 AM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle ("Waymo AV") operating in Santa Monica, California was in a collision involving an SUV on Maple Street near 10th Street. The Waymo AV was maneuvering out of a curbside parallel parking space at the curb on the south side of Maple Street when the passenger side front corner of the Waymo AV made contact with the driver side back corner of an SUV parked in front of the AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV's Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode. Both vehicles sustained damage. Print Clear Form OL 316 (REV. 7/2020) WWW
