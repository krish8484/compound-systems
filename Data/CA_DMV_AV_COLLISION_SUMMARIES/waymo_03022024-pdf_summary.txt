On March 2, 2024 at 8:14 AM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle ("Waymo AV") operating in San Francisco, California was in a collision involving a dumpster on Odd Fellows Way, at the rear garage exit of 1155 Market Street. The Waymo AV was making a reversing right turn out of the parking garage onto Odd Fellows Way when the rear left corner of the Waymo AV made contact with a dumpster which was next to the curb in the roadway opposite the garage exit. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV's Level 4 ADS was not engaged and a test driver was operating the Waymo AV in manual mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage.
