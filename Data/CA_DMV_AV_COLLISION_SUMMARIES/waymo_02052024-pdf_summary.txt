On February 5, 2024 at 10:01 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in Los Angeles, California was in a collision involving a gate at Watt Way and West Exposition Boulevard. The Waymo AV was traveling south on Watt when it approached an automatic gate as it had started to close. The right side of the Waymo AV made contact with the door of the gate. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage. Print Clear Form OL 316 (REV. 7/2020) WWW
