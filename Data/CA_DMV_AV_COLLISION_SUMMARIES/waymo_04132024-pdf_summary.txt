On April 13, 2024 at 3:35 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle ("Waymo AV") operating in San Francisco, California was in a collision involving a pickup truck on Bayshore Boulevard near Boutwell Street. The Waymo AV was traveling southbound on Bayshore Boulevard in the far right lane when it passed a pickup truck that was stopped in the bike lane. As the Waymo passed the pickup truck, the driver of the pickup truck opened the driver’s side door and the door made contact with the Waymo’s passenger side wing mirror and rear right sensor housing. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV's Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode, and a test driver was present (in the driver's seating position). The Waymo AV sustained damage.
